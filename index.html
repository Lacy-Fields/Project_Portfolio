Welcome to The Surly Sphynx!

Thanks for stopping by! We know how busy life can be, so we truely appreciate you taking time out of your day to drop in! The Surly Sphynx is a small passion project made by a couple, owned by their bald companions (not a typo, seriously these cats will kill you with kindness!), who wouldn't have it any other way. We hope to share our knowledge and first-hand experience with others who may have been looking for a surly companion for themselves!

Not sure where to start? Let's keep it simple! If you want to jump in, head over to our FAQ page! There, you'll find the spicy deets on these cool cats, whether you"re looking to learn or become forever loved! Just want to see cute Sphynx photos? Check out our Media page to see our fur(give or take some hairs) babies and others from the community! You're still reading!? Okay, okay! Learn more about who we are in our About tab and if you ever have questions, or just want to share more sweet Sphynx pics, @us on your social platform of choice!
